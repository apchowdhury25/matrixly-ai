# Policies

## Returns & refunds
Digital software pilots: cancel within 14 days of first invoice for a full refund if no production deploy was completed.
Custom implementation work is non-refundable once milestones are accepted in writing.

## Order status
For physical or fulfillment-related orders managed via Shipping Assistant:
1. Provide your order number (format ORD-#####)
2. We look up ShipStation / carrier tracking
3. We share ETA and exception notes

Without an order number we cannot guarantee shipment details and will escalate to ops.

## Privacy
We process support conversations to resolve your issue. We do not sell customer data. Conversation logs are retained for 90 days unless a longer period is required for an open ticket.

## Acceptable use
Do not use agents to send spam, harass individuals, or violate applicable law. Abuse may result in suspension.
