# Business Hours

## Support hours (America/Chicago)
- **Monday–Friday:** 9:00 AM – 6:00 PM CT
- **Saturday:** 10:00 AM – 2:00 PM CT
- **Sunday & US federal holidays:** Closed (chat bot still answers FAQs)

## Chat & email
- AI chat (SupportForge) is available 24/7 for common questions
- Live human escalation queue is staffed during support hours only
- After-hours escalations receive a same-next-business-day response target

## Holidays
We publish holiday closures on the website banner at least one week in advance.
