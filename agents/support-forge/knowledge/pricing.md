# Pricing

## SupportForge pricing (example SMB plan)
- **Starter:** $99/month — 1,000 chat conversations, knowledge base, email ingest, basic tickets
- **Growth:** $249/month — 5,000 conversations, HITL dashboard, usage/cost reports, priority support
- **Scale:** Custom — multi-site embeds, SSO, dedicated VPS, SLA

## Other Matrixly agents
Individual pilot agents are scoped per engagement. Contact sales for bundled marketplace pricing.

## Billing
Invoices are monthly in arrears for usage-based overages. Card payments and ACH supported. Cancel anytime before the next billing cycle.
