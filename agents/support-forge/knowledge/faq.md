# Frequently Asked Questions — Matrixly Demo Store

## What is Matrixly?
Matrixly is an agentic AI automation marketplace for SMBs. Ready-to-deploy agents help market, sell, ship, and run operations with human-in-the-loop controls.

## How do I contact support?
Email support@example.com or use the chat widget on our website. Typical first response is within one business day; chat often responds in minutes during business hours.

## Do you offer a free trial?
Yes. Most marketplace agents include a free exploration mode. Paid pilots and production deploys are available after a short onboarding call.

## Where can I see available agents?
Visit the Agents catalog at /agents.html on the Matrixly site. Featured agents include Lead Qualifier, Email Assistant, CRM Assistant, Shipping Assistant, and SupportForge.
