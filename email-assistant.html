// MAIL · TRIAGE · DRAFTS

Email Assistant

Triage your business inbox, draft replies for review, flag urgent items, and get a daily executive brief — so nothing important slips while you stay in control of every send.

1. Triage

Score unread mail and sort it into clear buckets — urgent, needs a reply, FYI, and more.

2. Flag urgent

Surface payments, deadlines, legal issues, VIP senders, and operational exceptions first.

3. Draft replies

Prepare reply drafts for your review. Nothing is sent until you approve.

4. Daily brief

Get a short executive summary of the last 24 hours so you can start the day focused.

How it works

Connect the agent to your business mailbox. It reviews unread messages, highlights what needs attention, drafts replies you can edit, and delivers a daily brief — without sending mail on its own.

Best for

  • Owners and ops leads who live in a busy shared or personal business inbox
  • Teams that want faster responses without auto-sending customer email
  • Anyone who wants a morning summary instead of digging through unread mail

Built-in guardrails

  • Draft-only by default — you approve before anything leaves the mailbox
  • Daily briefs go only to addresses you configure
  • Mailbox credentials stay in your environment; they are never part of the public site
